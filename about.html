Industrial Carbon Emission Audit — Textile

About this Project

Simple, practical carbon emission audit template tailored for the textile industry. This page explains objectives, method, and how to use the site.

Audit 2025

Overview

The Carbon Emission Audit helps identify and quantify CO₂e sources across the plant. It covers:

  • Fuel combustion (boilers, furnaces)
  • Electricity usage for processes
  • Transport and supply-chain emissions

Mission

To assist textile facilities in measuring their carbon footprint and finding cost-effective reduction opportunities — improving sustainability while maintaining productivity.

Objectives

  • Provide a clear emissions breakdown (Scope 1 / 2 / 3)
  • Offer actionable recommendations
  • Enable data-driven monitoring and reporting

Methodology

Data is collected monthly for fuels and electricity. Standard emission factors are applied to convert fuel/electricity usage into CO₂e. Results are aggregated to estimate annual emissions.

How to use

Go to Data to upload or view CSV records. Open Carbon Audit to see results and suggested interventions. Use this About page to understand purpose and process.
